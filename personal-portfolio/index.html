ABOUT ME

I graduated from Utica University in 2020 with a bachelors in forensic accounting and Hudson Valley Community College in 2022 with an associates in cybersecurity. I first got into coding in middle school where I exprimented with HTML and CSS by creating small websites for my friends. In my statistics class at Utica, we were briefly taught how to use Python for data mining for one of our projects. I realized I enjoyed analyzing big datasets and yearned to learn more.

Upon graduating, I realized that accounting was not for me and I wanted more of a tech-based career rather than finance/accounting. I started to become more interested in coding when I joined a project for a fan-made Pokemon game. I was fascinated by how the other devs on the project replicated my favorite series of all time. I went back to school for Cybersecurity in 2021. I learnt how to use Kali Linux and Java as well as Express.js. When I was not in school, I would brush up on my skills using Coursera, FreeCodeCamp, and Codeacademy.

I chose to pursue a career in tech because I enjoy the fact I could learn something new and the trial-and-error keeps me on my toes!
